# BloomX
BloomX is an interactive motivational device designed to inspire students in engineering and technology. Shaped as a flower that blooms when touched, it combines mechanical movement, ambient lighting, and digital displays to symbolize growth, resilience, and belonging. Beyond its symbolic design, BloomX functions as a personal task manager: users can track their goals, and upon completion, the device responds with positive feedback through blooming, lighting effects, and motivational messages on the OLED screen. The system integrates a servo-driven blooming mechanism, customizable RGB lighting controlled through a rotary encoder, and IoT connectivity for remote monitoring, customization, and interaction through a user-friendly dashboard making the device feel more engaging and supportive. By blending engineering with artistic design, and productivity features, BloomX goes beyond being just a prototype—it is a powerful reminder of empowerment, motivation, and creativity within STEM fields.
